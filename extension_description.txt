Vibe Extension - Send Prompts to Multiple AI Tools Simultaneously

Boost your AI workflow by sending the same prompt to multiple AI tools at once!

This extension allows you to:

• Write a prompt once and send it to multiple AI tools simultaneously
• Access pre-configured support for popular tools like Replit Ghostwriter, Bolt.new, and AI Agent Sourcebook
• Add your own custom AI targets with simple CSS selectors
• Save all your targets for future use

Perfect for:
• Comparing AI responses across different models
• Getting multiple AI opinions on your queries
• Testing the same prompt against different AI tools
• Setting up workflows that involve multiple AI assistants

Simple to use, with a clean, intuitive interface. All your settings are stored locally for privacy.

The extension requires tab and storage permissions to open tabs for each target AI tool and store your configurations.
